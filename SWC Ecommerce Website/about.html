Hi! My name is Paulina and I am a self-taught ceramist. Small waves is my ceramic studio based in Warsaw, Poland.

My work includes the whole variety of pottery such as cups, mugs, bowls, plates, vases, pots, palo santo holders etc. All pieces are handmade - thrown on a wheel, trimmed and glazed - and might vary in size, color and shape, which makes them unique.

Inspired by the nature, small waves is my trial to continue the culture of craftsmanship.
